Vacuum Casting Systems & Products

BMC and DIE Engineering (Thailand) Co., Ltd. (DET) started joint operations in South India in Oct 2023. DET, a Japanese brand based in Bangkok, delivers Vacuum Casting Systems, Chill Vents, Valve Blocks and related die solutions across Asia. Together we address die flow, gating optimization for vacuum, chill vent/block recommendations, and OEM-approved tool & die consultancy.

Operations

BMC markets, sells and services Vacuum Casting Systems, Chill Vents, Valve Blocks and tool/die related solutions with DET technical backing for Aluminium Foundries in Coimbatore, Bangalore & Chennai.

Solutions

  • Vacuum casting systems integration and commissioning
  • Chill vents and valve blocks selection aligned to flow characteristics
  • Die flow optimization and gating improvements for vacuum use
  • Approved consultancy for tools & dies with OEMs
