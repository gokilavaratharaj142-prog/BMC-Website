About Us

Best Marketing Company — trusted distributor for valves, crucibles, dosing systems, lubricants and automation equipment across India.

Reliability from selection to on-site commissioning

Established in 1994 in Coimbatore, operating across Tamil Nadu, Kerala and part of Karnataka. Focused on foundry and engineering segments with strong service ethos.

Foundry Products: Clay graphite, silicon carbide and refractory solutions for non-ferrous and ferrous applications.

Lubricants & Sealants: High temperature, machining coolants and speciality lubricants & impregnation sealants.

Automation: Aluminium foundry automation, Vacuum Impregnation Systems & Vacuum Casting Systems.
